## Credits System

Wizzx uses a credit-based pricing system:
- **1000 credits = $1 USD**
- Credits are deducted per API call based on the model and parameters used

### Flux 2 Megapixel Pricing

Flux 2 models use **Megapixel (MP)** based pricing where **Total MP = Input MP + Output MP**.

- **Output MP**: `width × height / (1024 × 1024)`, rounded up. Default 1 MP if not specified.
- **Input MP**: 0 if no images; each image = 1 MP for multiple images; actual size (max 4 MP) for single image.

**Flux 2 Pro:** 20 credits for first MP + 10 credits per additional MP
- Formula: `20 + (totalMP - 1) × 10`

**Flux 2 Flex:** 40 credits per MP
- Formula: `totalMP × 40`

| Scenario | Input MP | Output MP | Total MP | Pro Credits | Flex Credits |
|----------|----------|-----------|----------|-------------|--------------|
| Text-to-Image 1024×1024 | 0 | 1 | 1 | 20 | 40 |
| Text-to-Image 1920×1080 | 0 | 2 | 2 | 30 | 80 |
| Text-to-Image 2048×2048 | 0 | 4 | 4 | 50 | 160 |
| Image-to-Image (1 img 2MP + 1024×1024) | 2 | 1 | 3 | 40 | 120 |
| Image-to-Image (3 imgs + 1024×1024) | 3 | 1 | 4 | 50 | 160 |

## Cost Calculator

To calculate your cost:
1. Find the credits for your model and parameters above
2. Multiply by usage (e.g., seconds for video)
3. Divide by 1000 to get USD cost

**Example:** Generate a 10-second 720p video with Seedance Pro Fast
- Credits: 20 credits/s × 10s = 200 credits
- Cost: 200 ÷ 1000 = $0.20
